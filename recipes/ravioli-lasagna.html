Crockpot Ravioli Lasagna

The Lazy Lasagna for a Lazy Night In

This Crockpot Lasagna with Ravioli only requires 15 minutes of prep time. There are only 3 simple ingredients for a comforting, easy, and hearty meal that the whole family is going to go crazy for! It’s the perfect alternative to traditional lasagna when you’re pressed for time but still need to satisfy those saucy pasta cravings!

Ingredients

  • 1 package of frozen ravioli
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 8 oz shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 jar of spaghetti sauce
  • 1 diced yellow onion
